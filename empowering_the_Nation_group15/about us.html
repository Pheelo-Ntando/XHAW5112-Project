Welcome to Empowering the Nation

Empowering the Nation is a leading training provider dedicated to equipping individuals
with practical skills and knowledge to enhance their personal and professional lives.
Our comprehensive courses empower learners to become self-sufficient, confident, and resilient.

Our Story

Empowering the Nation was founded in 2018 by Precious Radebe, driven by a passion to
upskill individuals in her community who may not have had access to formal education or
training. Precious envisioned a platform where everyone could gain skills, knowledge,
and economic empowerment.

Our Mission

To support individuals in gaining new skills, becoming more employable, and improving
their overall quality of life.

Our Vision

A nation where individuals possess the skills and confidence to thrive in all aspects of life.

Our Values

Excellence in training delivery
Accessibility and inclusivity
Community engagement
Continuous improvement
